Recruitment 2023

Bengaluru 1st February 2024:
IMMEDIATE RELEASE

BMSIT Motor Heads Club Proudly Announces Recruitment of 23rd Batch

BMS Institute of Technology and Management (BMSIT) Motor Heads Club is thrilled to announce the recruitment of its 23rd batch, ushering in a new era of automotive enthusiasts and innovators. The Motor Heads Club at BMSIT has long been recognized as a pioneering hub for students passionate about automobiles, offering a platform for learning, collaboration, and hands-on experience in the dynamic world of automotive engineering and technology. With a legacy spanning over two decades, the Motor Heads Club continues to attract talented individuals who share a common zeal for all things automotive. This latest batch of recruits embodies the spirit of innovation and dedication that defines the club, showcasing a diverse range of skills and interests within the realm of automobiles. Members of the 23rd batch can look forward to engaging in a variety of activities and initiatives, including workshops, seminars, competitions, and community outreach programs. From designing and building electric vehicles to exploring the latest advancements in automotive technology, the club provides ample opportunities for students to expand their knowledge and make meaningful contributions to the field. "We are delighted to welcome the newest members of the Motor Heads family. "Each recruit brings a unique perspective and set of skills that will undoubtedly enrich our club's activities and further our collective passion for automobiles. We look forward to seeing the remarkable achievements and innovations that will undoubtedly emerge from this talented group." As the automotive industry continues to evolve and innovate, the Motor Heads Club remains at the forefront of empowering the next generation of engineers, designers, and leaders in the field. Through collaboration, creativity, and a shared love for all things automotive, the 23rd batch of recruits is poised to leave a lasting impact on the club and the industry as a whole.

For more information about the BMSIT Motor Heads Club and its activities.
